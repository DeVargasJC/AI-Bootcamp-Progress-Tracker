üéØ Your First Week Action Plan

Day 1: Setup (30 min)

  • Create accounts for Claude, ChatGPT, and Microsoft Copilot
  • Have a conversation with each - ask about your work challenges
  • Notice the differences in their responses

Day 2: Practice Prompting (30 min)

  • Ask for help with a real work task in data governance
  • Try different ways of phrasing the same question
  • Save the prompts that give you the best results

Day 3: Explore Tools (30 min)

  • Log into Microsoft Copilot Studio
  • Browse example agents and templates
  • Watch a quick tutorial video

Day 4: Build Something Small (30 min)

  • Create your first simple agent in Copilot Studio
  • Or compile 5 useful prompts for your daily work
  • Test and refine them

Day 5: Document & Reflect (30 min)

  • Write down what you learned this week
  • List 3 specific ways AI could help your work
  • Optional: Post on LinkedIn about your first week learning AI
